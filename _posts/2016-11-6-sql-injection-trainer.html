If you're a Web Designer/Developer and you don't really know much about security, Codebashing have a superb SQL Injection Demo, ideal for getting to grips with the basics of SQL Injection attacks and how to protect yourself when writing code that deals with SQL queries.

Codebashing infact offer a full suite of training tools aimed at developers and security experts alike. All of the training materials are game-based, similar to the training resources found on Treehouse.

If you happen to be a company looking to protect yourself against cyber-crime, then Codebashing offer an evaluation of their full suite of training materials, which you can request using the form on the Codebashing website.
